About me

Born and raised in Spokane, Washington, I am a student at Eastern Washington University and will be graduating in 2017 with a Bachelor of Art Degree in Visual Communication Design. I have been an artist for as long as I can remember. I look forward to all the new experiences that are ahead of me! Please feel free to contact me for any collaborations, questions, or business inquiries. Thanks!


Graphic designer, visual communicator and lover of all things art. I have been creating and expressing myself ever since I could pick up a crayon. Art has played a large role in my life whether it's sketching, drawing, painting, digital art, or photography. Art allows me to escape from the stress of every day life.
